About Me

I am a Doctoral Researcher at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU), focused on the intersection of Photonics and Artificial Intelligence. My current research involves developing Machine Learning-based frequency comb generation in Micro-Ring Resonators.

I previously served as a Junior Research Fellow at DRDO CHESS, where I pioneered Reinforcement Learning control frameworks for Tiled Aperture Coherent Beam Combining systems. My academic journey includes an M.S. from IIT Madras and a B.Eng from Anna University, with a consistent focus on applying Deep Learning to solve complex physical and scientific challenges in photonics and optical sensing.

Masters Thesis: Deep RL for Laser Arrays
