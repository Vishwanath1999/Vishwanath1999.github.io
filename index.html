About Me

I am a Doctoral Researcher at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) within the Department of Photonics. My research expertise lies at the intersection of Photonics and Machine Learning, specifically focusing on frequency comb generation in Micro-Ring Resonators.

With a strong foundation in Reinforcement Learning and Optical Sensing, I have previously worked at DRDO CHESS and IIT Madras on high-power laser systems and smart power grid applications. I am passionate about applying advanced AI architectures to solve complex scientific and physical problems.

Masters Thesis Highlight

Reinforcement Learning for Tiled Aperture Coherent Beam Combining

I developed a Deep Reinforcement Learning framework to control the phases of hundreds of laser beams for coherent combining. This research supports high-power laser applications including defense and nuclear fusion.
