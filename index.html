About Me

I am a Doctoral Researcher at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) within the Department of Photonics. My research focuses on Machine Learning based frequency comb generation in Micro-Ring Resonators. I am passionate about applying advanced AI architectures to solve complex scientific and physical problems.
